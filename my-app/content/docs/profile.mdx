Features
LiquidLink Profile is a self-sovereign identity platform that allows users to verify key identity attributes and link public contact channels such as GitHub and Twitter.
Profiles are fully editable and reflect the evolving nature of your on-chain presence.


### How to create your LiquidLink profile?
Creating your LiquidLink profile is as easy as three steps:
Connect your wallet to register.
Create your profile and add social links.
Approve the connection.
You're all set!
